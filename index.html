Mook's Defense of External Validity

A review written for Vokey's PSYC 3400 focused on the paper by Mook "In Defense of External Vality" (1983).

Overwatch Skill Rating

Attempting to determine which factors play a signficiant role in influence the individual performance statistic for Overwatchs skill rating calculations. A work in progress that I am just starting collecting data for.

The Abuse of Power

Article review looking at an article focused on the fallacious use of power calculations for data analysis by Hoenig and Heisey. Originally done for PSYC 3400 with John R. Vokey but later republished.

Review: Chow, S. L. (1987)

A review of an article published by Chow, S. L. in 1987 on the use of meta-analysis on pragmatic and theorical research. This was originally done for PSYC 3400 with John R. Vokey but has been changed and reposted.

Titanic Analysis

Predicting survival on the Titanic, using the dataset from Kaggle. Estimating the missing age variables using titles. Using Pclass to estimate the missing fare value and estimating values for embarked using the mode. Predictions were done using a binomial glm, but the primary focus was on data cleaning.
